# Project Brief - Deutsch B1 Exam
## Version 2.0

---

## 🎯 Core Goal
A premium Android application that prepares students for the German B1 language exam through realistic mock tests, interactive learning tools, graded reading stories, vocabulary flashcards, grammar drills, and gamified exercises — all in a stunning Glassmorphism dark-mode interface.

---

## 👥 Target Audience
- International students preparing for Goethe-Institut, ÖSD, or TELC B1 certification.
- Professionals seeking work visas in German-speaking countries.
- Language learners progressing from A2 to B1 who prefer a modern, mobile-first study environment over physical textbooks.

---

## 💎 Value Proposition
Consolidates mock exams, vocabulary practice, grammar drills, reading stories, and utility tools (dictionary, translator) into a single high-quality, offline-first Android app with a premium aesthetic that makes exam preparation feel engaging rather than stressful.

---

## 📦 Scope — Version 2.0

### In-Scope
- Mock tests: Goethe 1 & 2, ÖSD 1 & 2, TELC 1 (all 4 skills each)
- Result Summary Screen with answer review
- Room DB for exam score persistence and completion badges
- ExoPlayer audio integration for Hören screens
- Learn module: Grammar, Vocabulary, Geschichten (stories), Grammar Drills, Word Match + Fill-Blank Games
- WordVault: save any word from anywhere in the app; review as flashcards
- Progress Dashboard: streak, total study time, completion statistics
- Translation + Dictionary tools with error handling, loading states, and copy-to-clipboard

### Out-of-Scope (v2.0)
- User accounts / cloud sync
- Paid premium tiers
- Live video coaching
- B2-level content
